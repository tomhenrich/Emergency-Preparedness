# Car emergency/readiness kit

These supplies should be kept in a bag/box in the car at all times.

* First aid kit
* General supplies
  * Road flares (pack of 3)
  * Thick work gloves
  * Knee pads
  * Safety glasses
  * Toilet paper (1 roll)
  * Waste bags (1 roll)
  * Matches
  * Lighter
  * Replacement tire valve caps
  * N95 masks
  * Hand warmers
  * Reflective/hi-vis vest
